# B1F - Silent Approach

The onboarding floor: a winding labyrinth grown by rod-falling and cut through with
four chambers, teaching movement, combat, and searching. The stair is never locked —
the floor earns its exploration through reward and the difficulty below, not a gate.
The Warden's winch opens a sealed passage by the entrance, collapsing a return
descent to a few steps.
